Vision

The NBSC Foundation can take action on a wide variety of issues due to its unique character and the powers vested in its charter, which is considered as National Organisation. As such, the NBSC Character is an instrument of National Law, and NBSC Member Stated are bound by it. The NBSC Charter codifies the major principles of National & State relations.

Since the NBSC founding in 2021, the mission and work of the Organisation have been guided by the purposes and principles contained in its Founding Charter, which has been amended one time in 2021.
